📋 Team Standards

Do's and Don'ts

Our team standards — keeping us aligned and awesome.

✅ Do's — Best Practices
  • ✅Always log customer cases in Zendesk within 2 hours of contact.
  • ✅Use the shared brand asset folder for all creative deliverables.
  • ✅Respond to internal Slack messages within the same working day.
  • ✅Escalate blockers to the Team Lead before end of day — don't sit on them.
  • ✅Document decisions in the shared team notes page immediately after calls.
  • ✅Proofread all customer-facing copy before sending or publishing.
❌ Don'ts — Things to Avoid
  • ❌Don't share customer data outside secure, approved channels.
  • ❌Don't approve creative work without a QA sign-off from Jordan.
  • ❌Don't send client-facing emails without CC-ing the team inbox.
  • ❌Don't use personal accounts or shadow tools for team files or comms.
  • ❌Don't commit to external deadlines without checking team capacity first.
  • ❌Don't leave a case marked "pending" for more than 48 hours without an update.
